# Merchant Scenes

Use the scene to choose the strongest seeding angle and page plan.

## 亲子研学

- Users: parents, summer camp operators, study-trip institutions, museum education teams.
- Angle: "暑假不只是打卡, 给孩子一次可记录的观察任务".
- Pages: pain point, activity value, route/process, child gains, photo points, booking info, parent checklist.
- Avoid: promising academic improvement or exaggerating educational outcomes.

## 文博打卡

- Users: museums, exhibitions, cultural blocks, city route operators.
- Angle: "把展览变成一条好逛、好拍、好收藏的文化路线".
- Pages: why visit now, must-see highlights, route order, observation details, photo spots, nearby add-ons, saveable map.
- Avoid: inventing artifact facts or official claims.

## 非遗体验

- Users: lacquer fan, tie-dye, pottery, bamboo weaving, embroidery, incense, rubbing, paper craft studios.
- Angle: "不是普通手工课, 是能带走作品和照片的东方审美体验".
- Pages: finished product, process, materials, suitable groups, photo moments, booking notes, care tips.
- Avoid: generic museum-poster tone; show hands, tools, texture, and process evidence.

## 避暑旅行

- Users: ancient towns, mountain routes, gardens, guesthouses, family-friendly destinations.
- Angle: "低强度、好拍、能避暑的两天一夜/半日路线".
- Pages: cool reason, route, best time, family-friendly details, photo points, food/rest stop, saveable itinerary.
- Avoid: hiding travel friction; mention crowd, heat, reservation, or walking load when relevant.

## 茶香生活

- Users: tea spaces, tea drinks, tea brands, incense/fragrance brands, lifestyle stores.
- Angle: "把一杯茶/一支香变成夏日松弛感和东方生活方式".
- Pages: mood, product or menu, scene, detail, pairing, who it suits, visit/buy CTA.
- Avoid: vague Zen wording; use concrete taste, scent, object, and ritual.

## 汉服旅拍

- Users: hanfu rental, makeup, photography studios, cultural route photographers.
- Angle: "不做影楼古风, 做真实高级的城市文化旅拍".
- Pages: sample style, makeup detail, route, best light, package content, customer preparation, booking CTA.
- Avoid: plastic skin, palace-drama styling, fake location, over-promising results.
