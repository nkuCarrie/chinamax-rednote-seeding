# Guofeng Style System

Choose one primary style and one optional accent. Do not mix every guofeng symbol at once.

## 唐风华彩

Use for hanfu, makeup, jewelry, night tours, festival activities, Tang-themed museums, cultural blocks, and high-energy visual campaigns.

- Visuals: mineral pigments, mural-inspired color, silk, peony, warm architectural depth, ceremonial but not cluttered.
- Palette: vermilion as accent, malachite green, lapis blue, warm gold, ink black.
- Avoid: cheap red-gold, palace-drama poster, random dragons, heavy costume clutter.

## 宋式留白

Use for tea, incense, books, gardens, museums, family observation journals, quiet routes, guesthouses, and refined lifestyle services.

- Visuals: negative space, paper texture, celadon, ink gray, wood, window light, thin dividers.
- Palette: paper white, celadon, pine green, ink gray, walnut brown.
- Avoid: empty beige templates, fake calligraphy, weak contrast.

## 非遗手作

Use for craft workshops, handmade products, cultural classes, parent-child activities, local heritage stores.

- Visuals: close-up texture, hands, tools, process table, natural light, finished object.
- Palette: craft-dependent natural colors, cotton white, wood, clay, indigo, lacquer red as accent.
- Avoid: generic old-China symbols; show evidence of making.

## 节气东方

Use for seasonal products, summer trips, solar-term campaigns, tea, food, fragrance, flowers, wellness, lifestyle notes.

- Visuals: weather, plant, food, ritual object, seasonal light, small daily scene.
- Palette: seasonal but restrained; summer can use lotus green, tea white, bamboo, clear blue, pale yellow.
- Avoid: calendar cliche, random cranes, clouds, and lanterns.

## 东方极简

Use for premium products, fragrance, skincare, tea ware, cultural creative products, boutique guesthouses, design-led stores.

- Visuals: single object, shadow, stone, wood, paper, ceramic, low saturation, strong composition.
- Palette: off-white, black, stone gray, wood, one accent color.
- Avoid: cold luxury sameness; always include a tactile or cultural detail.
